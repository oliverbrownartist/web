DRiPPY

For 4+ performers with variable instrumentation, a photocopier, and at least one pair of scissors.
Graphic score; variable duration.


Description

As my Honours thesis project at the RMIT School of Art, DRiPPY used water as the abiding metaphor around which to assemble a multimedia exploration of vernacularity, humour, and networked interaction. DRiPPY culminated in two hybrid works: a generative kinetic installation with sculptural, sonic and text elements (installed June 2022), and a graphic, absurdist, text-based work which critiques the role of the academic exegesis in creative praxis. Both install documentation and selected pages from the graphic thesis are included in the gallery above.
