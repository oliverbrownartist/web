Creative Projects

My creative practice spans performance art, experimental sound, and interactive installations. The video below presents excerpts from multiple recent projects. For more detailed documentation and other projects, please navigate to individual project pages via the links below.


Selected Recent Projects

Xylocyclos (2024 - ongoing)

Site-specific performance project developed with Reed Wixson.
For two improvisers with native flora, electronic feedback, and household implements.
Field research conducted at Burns-Piñon Ridge Reserve, Yucca Valley, CA.

Dispense Me Some Score (2025)

Combined performance-installation project featuring two automatic paper handtowel dispensers, each loaded with rolls of hand-drawn graphic score.
For variable ensemble size and instrumentation.
First installed and performed at the Yes or NoTation exhibition, Kohta Taidehalli, Helsinki.

Burning Bush (2025)

Site-specific interactive installation. Salvaged timber and found objects are transduced in an immersive multichannel environment, responding via proximity data to the movements of observers.
First installed in May 2025 at the Transverse Range Art Showcase, Yucca Valley, CA.

Chop, Chop! (2024)

For two performers, four channels, kitchen utensils, and assorted vegetables.
Graphic score; 20 mins duration.
First performed with Reed Wixson at Monk Space, Koreatown, Los Angeles.

Three Supermarket Events (2024)

For voice, tape, live electronics, feedback, bass instrument, and hand percussion.
Graphic score; 20 mins duration.

Buzzcut (2024)

For solo performer with electric shaving clippers, voice, motion sensor, and live electronics.
Unscored; 9 mins duration.
First performed at the 2024 Listen to the Motion Festival, UCI.

Machine based context-sensitive system for managing multi-round dialog (2024)

Album-length release of free improvisation with Dr Isaac Otto (electric guitar) and Dr Chris Hadley (electronics, feedback drum, recording, mastering); I play amplified household objects.
Unscored; 58 mins duration.
Released on Unsound Recordings, Los Angeles, CA.

Step One: Photocopy This Score (2023)

For 4+ performers with variable instrumentation, a photocopier, and at least one pair of scissors.
Graphic score; variable duration.

Run, Chase (2023)

Solo performance with mounted ball-on-string, cricket bat and paraphernalia, motion sensor, and live electronics.
Unscored, 9 mins duration.
First performed at the 2023 Listen to the Motion Festival, UCI.

DRiPPY (2022)

Site-specific interactive installation with a dynamic system of vibrating objects suspended in amplified water vessels.
Installed at the RMIT University School of Art.

Alexa, Write Me Some Music (2021)

For solo cellist with interactive electronics.
Live-scored, 8 mins duration.
Commissioned by Dots+Loops New Music for the NONSTOP Festival.

Logical Conclusions (2021)

Durational performance artwork for solo performer with canvas, paint, amplification, and live electronics.
Live-scored; 16 hours duration.
Performed during Covid-19 lockdown in Naarm/Melbourne, Australia.

Music For Headphones (2021)

Recorded interactive work for solo listener.
Unscored, 5 mins duration.
Commissioned for EOROC Vol. 1 (Voltage Control Records, NY).

Velutha (2020)

For SATB saxophone quartet with household objects.
Animated score, 8 mins duration.
Awarded the 2020 Monash Composition Prize.
