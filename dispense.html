Dispense Me Some Score

Combined performance-installation project featuring two automatic paper handtowel dispensers, each loaded with rolls of hand-drawn graphic score.
For variable ensemble size and instrumentation.
First installed and performed at the Yes or NoTations> exhibition at Kohta Taidehalli (Helsinki, Finland), July 2025.


Description

This experimental graphic score project involves colourful graphic notation inscribed improvisationally by hand onto 200m long rolls of paper, and then loaded into two automatic hand-towel dispensers. Performers dispense themselves a new stretch of score as required, being instructed by turns to perform musical gestures; to move around the performance space; and to tear off and discard the existing score entirely. The piece’s flexible notation scheme has been designed for intuitive interpretation by ensembles of variable size, instrumentation, musical style, and performance experience.
